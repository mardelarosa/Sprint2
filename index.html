A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organize your favorite website. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favouriten websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

Bookmark in one click

Organize your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sites in no time at all. No need to trawi through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bookmarks

Easily share your bookmarks and collections with other. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button.
